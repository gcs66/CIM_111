ODESZA - A Moment Apart (Deluxe)

In ODESZA's most recent album, they take you on a journey from start to finish. After recently finishing their tour, Harrison and Clayton are back in the studio working on their next album.

Porter Robinson - Worlds

Although Worlds came out 5 years ago, it is still one of the most relevant albums around. Porter has been performing his Worlds show for years and is now looking forward to a new chapter.

Madeon - Adventure (Deluxe)

Madeon's debut album, Adventure, was released in March of 2015. This album has served as inspiration for artists around the world. Madeon's new album was released on November 15, 2019 and will help further Madeon's already successful career in music.

Zedd - Clarity (Deluxe)

As the first electronic album I ever listened to, Clarity holds a special place in my heart. Zedd's old sound was extremely unique and helped pave the way for future electronic music.
